# METAai — Authorship & Priority Statement

I, **Georgiy Bibilov (Георгий Бибилов)**, am the author and originator of the
**METAai Digital Organism Architecture** and the **Organism Harness Protocol**
described in this repository.

This public release establishes the authorship and the priority date of the
following concepts, as expressed in the documents, schemas and reference
implementation of this repository:

- **Digital Organism Architecture** for autonomous AI systems — agents and
  "organs" living in closed feedback loops (sense → reason → propose → act →
  learn) rather than as one-shot prompt chains.
- **Organism Harness Protocol** — a contract that turns any system/agent into a
  governed element: capabilities, permissions, events, health, rollback,
  human gate, policy.
- **Harness-of-Harnesses / Meta-System** layering — governing many harnessed
  systems through a shared control plane.
- **Meta-Field interaction layer** — systems coordinate by changing a shared
  field of reality (facts, goals, risks, attention, economy, trust) instead of
  direct point-to-point commands.
- **Meta-Constitution** for AI organisms — prime directives, forbidden actions,
  and graduated autonomy levels (observe → suggest → simulate → prepare →
  execute-reversible → execute-irreversible → self-modify).
- **Decision Ledger + Human Gate** governance pattern — every consequential
  decision is recorded with rationale, confidence and outcome; irreversible
  actions require explicit human approval.
- **Rejection Re-examiner ("anti-mirror in time")** — rejected proposals mature
  and are re-challenged from the height of new reality, without spamming the
  human.
- **Internal Task Market** and **Reputation / Evolutionary Selection** for AI
  agents and organs — usefulness, cost and risk decide what is amplified,
  paused or retired.
